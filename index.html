BassWalk

This is a tool made for (but not limited to) practicing walking bass lines. I like challenging myself to combine weird sequences and explore which walks work well.

First: There is a metronome. The speed can be controlled by entering bpm into the box in the navigation bar.

The green box shows the current chord, the red box shows the upcoming chord. Under the chord name an arrow indicates whether to walk up or down to the next chord. Optionally (flip the "Interval/Just direction" switch), it can also randomly display a T or Q, showing whether to make the walk third or fifth based.

Instead of random chord sequences you can also flip the "random/predefined" switch and provide a comma separated list of chords to cycle through.
